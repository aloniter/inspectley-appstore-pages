What Inspectley Does

Inspectley is an iOS app designed for building inspectors and field professionals. Create inspection projects, capture photos directly from the camera or your photo library, annotate images with markers and notes, and produce polished PDF or DOCX reports ready to share with clients.

  • Organize inspections into projects with structured data
  • Capture or import inspection photos and annotate them in-app
  • Export professional PDF and DOCX reports
  • Full Hebrew (RTL) language support
  • All inspection data stored privately on your device
