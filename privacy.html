Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Inspectley (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the app.

Account & Authentication Data

To use Inspectley you must create an account. We collect your email address for authentication purposes via our third-party authentication provider, Supabase. This is the only personal information collected during registration.

Company & Profile Data

If you configure company branding (such as a company name, logo, or contact information for export headers), that data is stored in our backend (Supabase) so it can be applied to your exported reports across sessions.

Export Authorization & Subscription Status

Information about your export permissions and trial/subscription status is stored in our backend (Supabase) to manage access to export features.

Inspection Data (Stored Locally on Your Device)

All inspection projects, reports, descriptions, notes, annotations, and photos you create in Inspectley are stored locally on your device only. This data is not uploaded to our servers or to Supabase in the current version of the app.

2. Camera & Photo Library Access

Inspectley requests access to the following device capabilities:

  • Camera — used to capture inspection photos directly within the app.
  • Photo Library — used to import existing photos from your device into an inspection project.

Photos you capture or import are stored locally on your device. They are not transmitted to any server.

3. How Exports Work

You can export your inspection reports as PDF or DOCX files. Exports are generated on your device and shared using iOS's standard share sheet (AirDrop, email, Files, etc.). Report files are not uploaded to Inspectley's servers during this process.

4. Third-Party Services

Inspectley uses Supabase as a backend service provider. Supabase processes the following data on our behalf:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Company branding and profile data
  • Export authorization and trial/subscription status

Supabase's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, available at supabase.com/privacy.

We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not display advertisements or use advertising networks.
  • We do not track you across third-party apps or websites.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.
  • We do not upload your inspection photos, reports, or local project data to our servers (in the current version of the app).
  • We do not use external payment links or redirect you to third-party payment pages outside of the App Store.

6. Data Retention

Account data (email, company branding, and export permissions) is retained as long as your account is active. Local inspection data on your device is under your full control; you may delete any project or photo at any time within the app. If you wish to delete your account and associated backend data, please contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Inspectley is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at the email address below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
