Common Questions

Where is my inspection data stored?
All your inspection projects, photos, and reports are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to the cloud in the current version of the app.

How do I export a report?
Open a project, tap the export button, choose PDF or DOCX, and use the iOS share sheet to send or save the file wherever you'd like.

I can't log in — what should I do?
Make sure you're using the email address you registered with. If the problem persists, email us at iteralon@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.

How do I delete my account?
Send us an email at iteralon@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will process your request promptly.
